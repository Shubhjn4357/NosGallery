**Grand Total: 550 Widgets**

For NOS Gallery, I'd actually bundle ~250 high-quality widgets initially and keep the remaining 300+ as downloadable packs. Shipping 550 widgets on day one turns your app into a warehouse. Users don't need to scroll through 550 cards to find a clock. They will simply install the first one, admire it for six minutes, and move on to customizing it for the next three hours. 😌📱
